LaunchPad Dinner with Chef Laura Cole

AUGUST 23 at 6:00

Join Ah Haa in the Launchpad for a seven-course menu that highlights Alaskan cuisine, including King Crab, Black Cod and Reindeer, complete with wine pairings. Chef Laura Cole has been defining Alaskan cuisine since the 2005 opening of 229 Parks Restaurant and Tavern in Denali National Park & Preserve, which she ran for 18 years. A Top Chef Season 15 participant (which was filmed in Denver, Boulder, Aspen and Telluride).

RESERVE YOUR SEATS

LAUNCHPAD DINNER WITH CHEF TANYA HOLLAND

OCTOBER 25 at 6:00

Join Ah Haa in the Launchpad for a soulful multi-course harvest dinner with award winning chef, author, restaurateur and Top Chef Season 15 competitor Tanya Holland! Known for her inventive take on modern soul food, as well as comfort classics, Tanya Holland is the founder of Oakland, California’s famed Brown Sugar Kitchen restaurant. She currently sits on the Board of Trustees for The James Beard Foundation and is the Chef/Chair of its Awards Committee.

Providing Arts Access & Opportunity!

A community hub for creative discovery and exploration for over 30 years, Ah Haa offers a wide range of classes, workshops and activities for people of all ages and backgrounds to express their potential through art. Offerings include Ceramics, Painting, Drawing, Textile Arts, Jewelry, Culinary Arts, and more!
